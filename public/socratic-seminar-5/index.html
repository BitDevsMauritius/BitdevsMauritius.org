Topics

Lightning Network Fundamentals + Bitcoin & Quantum Considerations

Duration: 1 Hour 5 Minutes Audience: Developers, CS students, security-focused builders

Meetup Agenda

1. Opening & Context (5 minutes)

  • Welcome and session framing
  • Why Lightning Network matters for Bitcoin scalability
  • Introducing the idea of long-term security (including quantum considerations)

2. Lightning Network — Beginner Foundations (25 minutes)

Objective: Build a clear mental model of Lightning

  • What the Lightning Network is and why it exists
  • Payment channels: opening, updating, closing
  • Off-chain vs on-chain transactions
  • HTLCs (Hashed Time-Locked Contracts) simplified
  • Routing payments across the network

Discussion:

  • How Lightning improves speed and cost
  • Trade-offs and limitations

3. Bitcoin & Quantum Considerations (20 minutes)

Objective: Introduce future-facing security thinking

  • What quantum computing is (high-level)
  • Why it matters for cryptography in Bitcoin
  • Potential risks to elliptic curve cryptography
  • Current state: theoretical vs practical threat

Reference Discussion:

  • Insights from bitcoin-quantum
  • Possible mitigation paths (quantum-resistant signatures, migration paths)

4. Open Discussion & Builder Direction (15 minutes)

  • Should Bitcoin developers care about quantum today?
  • How to start exploring Lightning development
  • Pathways into research and contribution
  • Btrust Builder Pathways (5 minutes)
  • Btrust Builders
