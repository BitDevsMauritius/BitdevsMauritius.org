Topics

Encryption & A Deep Dive into Nostr Relays

Duration: 1 Hour

Speakers: Hanif (Coordinator), Bruno Bernard (Main Speaker)

Meetup Agenda

1. Opening & Context (5 minutes)

Speaker: Hanif

  • Welcome and session overview
  • Why encryption matters in decentralized systems
  • Introduction to Nostr and its relevance

2. Encryption Fundamentals in Nostr (15 minutes)

Speaker: Bruno Bernard

  • Public/private key cryptography in Nostr
  • How identities are derived from keys
  • Event signing and verification
  • Basic encryption model used in Nostr communication

3. Nostr Relay Architecture Deep Dive (25 minutes)

Speaker: Bruno Bernard

  • What a relay is and its role in the protocol
  • Client-relay interaction model
  • Event propagation and storage
  • Relay policies, censorship resistance, and trade-offs
  • Security considerations in relay design

4. Practical Discussion & Use Cases (10 minutes)

Speaker: Bruno Bernard

  • How developers can interact with relays
  • Common tools and implementations
  • Real-world use cases and challenges

5. Open Q&A and Closing (5 minutes)

Speaker: Hanif

  • Questions and discussion
  • Key takeaways
  • Next meetup preview
  • Btrust Builders
